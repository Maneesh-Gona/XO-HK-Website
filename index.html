Become
Inspired
Chi Omega is group of excited and passionate young women who strive everyday to live our lives based on the principles Chi Omega has taught us. Founded in 1979, we are proud to call Eta Kappa our home. We hope to share our principles for which we stand, our rich tradition and everlasting friendships that are developed based on the bonds of sisterhood.
Friendship
Friendship is what binds us to each other and to our Fraternity. The friendships we develop in Chi Omega give us a home across geography and generations and give us lasting support throughout our lives.
Sisterhood
Sisterhood means that we are women of integrity and good character. We have high expectations for ourselves and for our sisters. We strive daily to be role models to others.
Service
Service means that we want to make a difference on our campuses and in our communities. We know that giving back to others unselfishly makes the world a better place and makes us better people. Serving and giving helps to connect us.

Participation
Participation develops us into well-rounded people. We engage with others to develop outside interests and expose us to new and differing points of view. By contributing in the larger world, we demonstrate that Chi Omega makes a difference in the lives of women.
Sincere Learning
Sincere Learning means that we care about our intellectual development. We embrace the spirit and excitement of learning throughout our lives and encourage others to better themselves.
