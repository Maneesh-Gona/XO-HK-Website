Chi Omega is group of excited and passionate young women who strive everyday to live our lives based on the principles Chi Omega has taught us. Founded in 1979, we are proud to call Eta Kappa our home.

We hope to share our principles for which we stand, our rich tradition and everlasting friendships that are developed based on the bonds of sisterhood.
