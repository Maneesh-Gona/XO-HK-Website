Welcome Potential New Members!

Formal recruitment at Missouri S&T is an exciting and rewarding experience. The week of recruitment is designed so that undergraduate women have the opportunity to visit all three panhellenic chapters on our campus. As a Chi Omega, you will make fond memories, develop a foundation for leadership, and meet the best friends you will always treasure. You will also become a part of the strong Greek community on campus. Greeks are leaders on our campus, within our communities and for our nation. This community and our Chi Omega chapter provide endless opportunities for involvement and success. Beyond that, Chi Omega provides a sisterhood and a home. You can make a difference in Chi Omega, and Chi Omega can make a difference in you.

We are so excited for you to join the Panhellenic community here at the Missouri S&T, and we look forward to meeting you at recruitment! To register for fall recruitment 2017 and learn more information, click the box below and you will be directed to the National Panhellenic Conference webpage.
